Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

IIB Smart Mobile Banking ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.

1. Information We Process

The App may process certain user information strictly as required to provide banking and related services. This may include:

  • Login and authentication data
  • Phone number and identity-related information
  • KYC information and documents submitted by the user
  • One-time password (OTP) data for verification and security
  • Basic device and technical information necessary for app functionality, security, and compatibility

2. Purpose of Processing

We process the above information only for legitimate service-related purposes, including:

  • User authentication and secure login
  • Identity verification and KYC compliance
  • OTP generation and verification
  • Providing requested banking services
  • Fraud prevention, security monitoring, and service protection
  • Improving reliability, compatibility, and performance of the App

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell user personal data.

We do not use user data for advertising.

We do not share personal information with third parties except when necessary for:

  • Providing the requested service
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Compliance with legal, regulatory, or banking obligations
  • Supporting regulated banking operations through authorized service providers or official systems

4. Data Storage and Retention

User data is retained only as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, security, and regulatory requirements. Some information may be processed temporarily within the App for authentication and transaction-related purposes.

5. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. User Rights

Users may contact us regarding questions about their personal information, subject to applicable laws and banking regulations.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
